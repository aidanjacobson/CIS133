Introduction

I have been playing the viola since I was in 3rd grade,which comes out to almost 11 years. During that time, I progressed from not even knowing how to use the bow, to performing onstage at Carnegie Hall.


My Experience

Music

Throughout the years, I have played quite a few pieces of music. For example, I right now I am learning to play Élégie for Viola and Piano, written by Henri Vieuxtemps in 1893. Some of my favorite pieces include the ones we played at Carnegie Hall, which include Serenade for Strings in C Major by Pyotr Ilyich Tchaikovsky, Hoedown from Rodeo by Aaron Copeland, and Intermezzo from Cavalleria Rusticana by Pietro Mascagni (you might recognize this pieces from its use in The Godfather III if you make it through the first and second one [I did not]). Opportunities

In my time playing, I have had the opportunity to perform in some really cool settings. Here are some of the unique opportunities being a musician has afforded me:

  • Performing at Carnegie Hall during the 2018 New York Invitational Music Festival
  • Performing for an adjudication at Hershey Park
  • Performing alongside Maestro Zhang Yue in the SUSD Honor Orchestra in 2018
  • Receiving the Arizona State Seal of Fine Arts Proficiency upon graduating in 2020
